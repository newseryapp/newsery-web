What News App Is the Most Unbiased? (And What "Unbiased" Really Means)

Newsery Editorial · Feb 25, 2026

"Unbiased" is one of the most searched qualities in news apps. But it is also one of the most misunderstood.

What People Mean When They Search for Unbiased News

When people search for an unbiased news app, they usually mean one of a few things: they want diverse perspectives, they want less sensationalism, they want to avoid sources they distrust, or they want the algorithm to stop pushing emotionally charged content.

Why True Algorithmic Neutrality Is Impossible

Every feed involves choices: which sources to include, how to rank stories, what counts as relevant. There is no truly neutral algorithm. The question is not whether a system has a perspective — it always does — but whether that perspective is transparent.

What You Can Actually Control

What matters more than "unbiased" is control. If you can see what sources are included, choose your own topic weights, and decide what you prioritize — then the feed reflects your judgment, not an algorithm's.

How Newsery Handles This

Newsery does not claim to be unbiased. Instead, it gives you control: you choose the categories, you set the weights, and every article clearly shows its source. You decide what to trust. The system gives you structure — not a hidden agenda.
